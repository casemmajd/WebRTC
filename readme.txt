  Download to your machine
  Launch the index.html on your machine
  Copy the room number (the random string after # in the URL) and add to "https://scaledrone.github.io/webrtc/index.html#"
  Send the "https://scaledrone.github.io/webrtc/index.html#3c37a" to other party for use
  Allow the browser to use your camera and microphone
  Refresh the browser on both sides
  The call shoul come up
